Who We Are

The Georgia Tech Chamber Choir is Georgia Tech's foremost choral ensemble. Performing mainly contemporary choral music by greats such as Eric Whitacre, they also occasionally perform more classical choral works such as The First Tears by Erik Eschenvalts. The Georgia Tech Chamber Choir is most known for performances with the GT Orchestra with memorable pieces like Novel Sphere and Brahms Requiem.

The Chamber Choir also goes on a yearly tour. Some recent touring locations include San Diego, New York, and Atlanta.
